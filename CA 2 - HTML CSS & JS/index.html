Everybody comes to Rick's

Café American is a WWII tale of tragedy, intrigue, espionage and romance. Director Martin Scorsese reimagines this timeless American classic staying true to the original play ‘Everybody Comes to Rick’s’ by Murray Burnett.

"Not an easy day to forget"

One of the best movies out this year, the director has produced a love story entangled in war combined with superb performances delivered from the start. The remake of Casablanca does not disappoint the audience with the right balance between historical authenticity and a modern twist. Tipped to be the film of the year. Scorcese delivers another classic.
